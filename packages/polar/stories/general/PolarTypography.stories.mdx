# PolarTypography

`PolarTypography` draws text on a polar chart.

<ArgsTable of={PolarTypography} />

## Position

By default, `PolarTypography` creates text at the origin.
Prop `position` specifies an alternate position in the form of a radius (in pixels) and an angle (in degrees).

## Orientation

`PolarTypography` orient text horizontally by default.
Flags `radial` and `tangential` rotate the labels based on their position along the circle.

## Variants

The default variant for `PolarTypography` is `polar-label`.
The characteristic of this variant is that it automatically rotates some labels:
radial labels are rotated differently in the left and right hemispheres;
tangential labels are rotated different in the top and bottom hemisphere.

Note that labels in the right and left hemispheres may require different alignments.
That can be achieved through themes.
