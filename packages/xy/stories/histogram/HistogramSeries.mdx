# HistogramSeries

<Meta of={HistogramSeriesStories} />

`HistogramSeries` is a convenience component that combines the capabilities of `HistogramArea`, `HistogramBars`, and `HistogramCurve`.

<ArgTypes of={HistogramSeriesStories} />

## Layers

The prop `layers` controls the amount of information that is displayed about a series.
Setting the prop to `['area', curve']` displays an area polygon and a curve.
