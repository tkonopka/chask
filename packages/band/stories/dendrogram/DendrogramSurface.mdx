# DendrogramSurface

<Meta of={DendrogramSurfaceStories} />

`DendrogramSurface` draws rectangles around dendrogram branches.

<ArgTypes of={DendrogramSurfaceStories} />

## Levels

By default `DendrogramSurface` draws a series of rectangles, each capturing one layer of the dendrogram's hierarchy.

<Canvas of={DendrogramSurfaceStories.AllLevels} />

Prop `levels` accepts am array of integers and draws rectangles around specific parts of the dendrogram.
Integers are interpreted as (off-by-one) indexes in the `merge` field in the original data.

<Canvas of={DendrogramSurfaceStories.SelectedLevels} />

## Data subsets

Props `ids` offers an alternative method to draw surface.
This prop takes a list of leaf identifiers, and creates a surface around the minimal hierarchy branch that encompasses those leaves.

<Canvas of={DendrogramSurfaceStories.Subset} />

## Expansion

Props `expansion` enlarges the surface around a branch.
