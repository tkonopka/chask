# Slice

<Meta of={SliceStories} />

`Slice` draws one piece of a pie / doughnut chart.

`Slice` components are typically not used as standalone objects, but rather used as a prop to `Slices`.

<ArgTypes of={SliceStories} />

## Position and size

A slice is defined by two radii (props `innerRadius` and `outerRadius`)
and two angles (props `startAngle` and `endAngle`).
The angles can be specified in degrees or radians, the unit declared in prop `angleUnit`.

<Canvas of={SliceStories.QuarterCircleDegrees} />
<Canvas of={SliceStories.HalfCircleRadians} />

## Corners

Prop `r` sets a radius for rounded corners.

<Canvas of={SliceStories.RoundedCorners} />

## Padding

Prop `padAngle` shrinks the effective shape to create empty space at the slice boundaries.
The examples below display semi-circular slices.
Non-zero `padAngle` values make the slices appear slightly smaller.
