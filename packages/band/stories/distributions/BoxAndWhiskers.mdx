# BoxAndWhiskers

<Meta title="Addons/Band/Distributions/BoxAndWhiskers" />

`BoxAndWhiskers` draws a rectangle and whiskers to summarize a distribution.

<ArgTypes of={BoxAndWhiskers} />

`BoxAndWhiskers` is not typically used on its own.
Rather, it is passed to prop `component` of the `Distributions` component.
