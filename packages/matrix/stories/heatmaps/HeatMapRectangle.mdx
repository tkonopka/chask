<ArgTypes of={HeatMapRectangle} />

## Example

`HeatMapRectangle` is closely related to `HeatMapSimpleRectangle`.
It is intended to be used in `HeatMapCells` to draw a heat map.

<Canvas of={HeatMapRectangleStories.Example} />

In contrast to `HeatMapSimpleRectangle`, `HeatMapRectangle` provides animation for the color fill.
Color animations can make heatmap updates appear smoother.
However, in large visualizations with hundreds of cells, animations may decrease responsiveness.
