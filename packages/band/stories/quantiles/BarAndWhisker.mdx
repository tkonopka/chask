# BarAndWhisker

<Meta title="Addons/Band/Quantiles/BarAndWhisker" />

`BarAndWhisker` draws a rectangle and a whisker to summarize a distribution.

<ArgTypes of={BarAndWhisker} />

`BarAndWhisker` is not typically used on its own.
Rather, it is passed to prop `component` of the `Distributions` component.
