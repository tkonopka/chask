# LineAndWhiskers

<Meta title="Addons/Band/Distributions/LineAndWhiskers" />

`LineAndWhiskers` draws lines to summarize a distribution.

<ArgTypes of={LineAndWhiskers} />

`LineAndWhiskers` is not typically used on its own.
